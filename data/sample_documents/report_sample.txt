QUARTERLY FINANCIAL REPORT - Q4 2025

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The company achieved strong growth in Q4 2025, with revenue increasing 23% year-over-year to $4.2 million. Operating margins improved to 18%, driven by operational efficiency gains and successful product launches.

KEY METRICS
- Revenue: $4.2M (+23% YoY)
- Gross Margin: 62% (+3pp)
- Operating Margin: 18% (+5pp)
- Customer Acquisition: 340 new clients
- Retention Rate: 94%
- Employee Count: 127 (+15 net)

HIGHLIGHTS
The launch of our AI-powered analytics platform exceeded expectations, generating $800K in new revenue. Customer satisfaction scores reached an all-time high of 4.7/5.0. International expansion into Brazil and Colombia contributed 12% of total revenue.

RISKS AND CONCERNS
Market competition is intensifying in the enterprise segment. Two major competitors launched similar products at lower price points. Currency fluctuation in Latin American markets poses a moderate financial risk. The deadline for regulatory compliance with new data protection laws is March 2026.

OUTLOOK
We project Q1 2026 revenue of $4.8M based on current pipeline and seasonal trends. Key initiatives include expanding the AI product suite, entering the Chilean market, and achieving SOC2 Type II certification.

RECOMMENDATIONS
1. Accelerate AI product development to maintain competitive advantage
2. Implement hedging strategy for LATAM currency exposure
3. Prioritize SOC2 certification before the March deadline
4. Increase sales team capacity by 20% to support growth targets
